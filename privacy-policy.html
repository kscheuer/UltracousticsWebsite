Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Ultracoustics Technologies Ltd. ("Ultracoustics," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website ultracoustics.ca. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with us through our contact form.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

We do not collect personal information automatically. This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or any other form of automated data collection.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use your information for marketing purposes, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of Ultracoustics Technologies Ltd.

3. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form handling service. When you submit the contact form, your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers to deliver the submission to us via email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, page requested) as part of their infrastructure. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, local storage, or any client-side tracking technologies. We do not use Google Analytics or any other analytics platform.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain business records. We do not maintain a separate database of contact form submissions.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at kyle@ultracoustics.ca.
